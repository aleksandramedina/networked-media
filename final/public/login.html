Those hobbies you never tried because of lack of time? Those books you never read? Movies you never watched? There is literally no better time than now to finally get to those things. But if you are stuck in a world of endless possiblities, here is a tool to explore new things to try out and document them as you go along to make sure this strange time stays in your memory. So let's appreciate the fact that we're in a position where we can use this time for self development and get out of quarantine a better version of ourselves!
